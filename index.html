About

My name is Vasileios Triglianos and I am a PhD student in the Faculty of Informatics at the University of Lugano. My advisor is Prof. Cesare Pautasso. My research interests involve MOOCs, software clicker systems and everything revolving around web technologies. In the past, I conducted research on Networked Virtual Environments.

And yes, this is the basic bootstrap template :-)
